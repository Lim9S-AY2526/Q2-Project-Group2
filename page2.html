Offered Grade Levels at OBMC



Casa (Pre-Kindergarten to Kindergaten)

The Casa Program helps children ages 3 to 5 develop independence and intelligence through hands-on learning.



Primary (Grades 1 to 3)

The Primary Grade School Program helps students ages 6 to 8 grow intellectually, physically, and independently.



Intermediate (Grades 4 to 6)

The Intermediate Grade School Program builds academic skills, teamwork, and leadership in students ages 9 to 12.



Junior High School (Grades 7 to 10)

The Junior High Program prepares students ages 13 to 16 for independence through social, emotional, and skill development.



Senior High School (Grades 10 to 12)

The Senior High Program helps students ages 17 to 18 gain skills for college and future careers.
